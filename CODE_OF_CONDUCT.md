# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please keep this project respectful, constructive, and privacy-aware.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and kind.
- Focus on reproducible technical discussion.
- Redact private slide content before sharing screenshots or logs.
- Report bugs without exposing secrets, tokens, cookies, or customer materials.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Publishing private presentation content without permission
- Posting credentials, sensitive data, or doxxing material

## Maintainer Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove content that violates these expectations.
